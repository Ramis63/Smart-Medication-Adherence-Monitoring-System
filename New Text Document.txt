System confirmation
Heart rate measurement
·Measures heart rate in BPM (beats per minute)
·Uses MAX30102 sensor via I2C
·Displays BPM in console and logs
Temperature sensor
·DS18B20 temperature sensor included
·Measures temperature in °C
·Displays temperature in console and logs
Buzzer system
·One buzzer handles all alerts:
·Medication reminders
·Temperature abnormalities
·Heart rate abnormalities
LED indicators
·Green LED (Pin 15) near heart sensor: blinks with buzzer when HR abnormal
·Red LED (Pin 16) near temp sensor: blinks with buzzer when temp abnormal
·Yellow/Blue LED (Pin 18) near button:
·Blinks during medication alarm
·Turns ON for 3 seconds when medication taken (with buzzer beep)
Medication tracking
·Button turns off alarm and confirms intake
·When medication taken: LED ON + buzzer beeps for 3 seconds
·Optional vitals measurement after intake
